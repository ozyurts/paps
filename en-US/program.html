Program Flow

How pilot support works

The flow is designed to listen without judgment and to offer practical support quickly.

1. First Contact

A pilot reaches out voluntarily via a secure support channel.

2. Peer Conversation

A suitable peer supporter connects, listens, and helps structure next steps.

3. Follow-up & Referral

When needed, additional check-ins and guidance to professional care are provided.
